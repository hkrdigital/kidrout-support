Kidrout Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

Kidrout respects your privacy.

All child profiles, routines, tasks, rewards, and statistics are stored locally on the user's device.

If iCloud synchronization is enabled, data is securely stored within the user's personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit services.

Kidrout does not sell personal information and does not share user data with third parties.

Profile photos selected by the user are used only for app functionality and are never transmitted outside Apple's services.

Kidrout does not require account creation or user registration.
