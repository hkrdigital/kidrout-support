Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2026

Overview

Kidrout respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Kidrout app handles information used inside the app.

Information stored in the app

Kidrout may store child profiles, routines, tasks, rewards, statistics, badges, selected profile photos, and app settings.

Local storage and iCloud

App data is stored locally on the user’s device. If iCloud synchronization is enabled, app data may be securely stored and synchronized through the user’s personal iCloud account using Apple’s CloudKit services.

Profile photos

Profile photos selected by the user are used only for app functionality. They are not sold or shared with third parties by Kidrout.

Subscriptions and purchases

Kidrout offers optional Premium features through Apple In-App Purchases and subscriptions. Purchases are processed by Apple. Kidrout does not receive or store payment card details.

Advertising

The free version of Kidrout may display ads. Advertising may be provided through Google AdMob. Ad providers may use device-related data according to their own privacy policies and applicable consent settings.

Notifications

Kidrout may use local notifications for reminders. These notifications are scheduled on the device and can be controlled by the user in iOS Settings.

Data sharing

Kidrout does not sell personal information. Kidrout does not require account creation or user registration.
